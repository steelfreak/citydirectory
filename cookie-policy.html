Cookie Policy and Consent

Effective Date: June 1, 2025

Welcome to Gulu City Directory (“City Directory,” “we,” “us,” or “our”)! Like many websites, we use cookies and similar technologies on dgulu.com (the “Website”) to make your experience smoother, more personalized, and secure. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how and why we use them, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Think of cookies as tiny digital helpers — small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit websites. They remember useful details about your visit, like your preferences, so that the next time you come back, the site feels more tailored to you.

2. The Cookies We Use

We use different kinds of cookies to make the Website work better for you:

  • Essential Cookies: Crucial for things to function smoothly. They enable basic operations like secure login and remembering your privacy choices.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how you use the Site — which pages you visit, and if anything goes wrong — so we can improve your experience.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your choices such as language or region, giving you a personalized visit every time.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: Track your browsing patterns to show you ads that really match your interests, sometimes with the help of trusted advertising partners.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Recognize you when you return to our Website, so you don’t have to start from scratch.
  • Keep things running smoothly and improve Website performance.
  • Serve ads that are meaningful and relevant to you — both here and on other websites.
  • Gather insights on how visitors use the Website, helping us to make smart improvements.
  • Protect against fraud and keep your data safe.

4. What About Third-Party Cookies?

Sometimes, we let third-party partners (like marketers and analytics services) place cookies on your device. These external cookies allow them to understand your interests across different websites and show ads that fit your tastes.

Please note: We don’t control these third-party cookies, so be sure to check their privacy policies if you want to understand more.

5. Your Choices — Take Control of Your Cookies

You’re in charge! Here’s how you can manage cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies. Remember, if you disable them, some features on our Website might not work perfectly.
  • Opt-Out from Ads: You can opt out of targeted advertising by visiting self-regulation websites like the Network Advertising Initiative (NAI) or Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA).
  • Cookie Consent Tools: When you visit dgulu.com, you might see a cookie banner or similar option letting you accept or decline non-essential cookies. Feel free to adjust your preferences there.

6. Updates to This Policy

We’re always working to improve. When technology or laws change, we may update this Cookie Policy. Any changes will be posted here with a new effective date, so please check back from time to time.
