Welcome to Gulu City: The Heart of Northern Uganda

Nestled in the rolling landscapes of Northern Uganda, Gulu City is a vibrant and fast-growing urban center rich with culture, history, and opportunity. As the largest city in the Northern Region, Gulu blends tradition and modernity, making it a must-visit destination and a hub of commerce, education, and innovation. Whether you are a visitor, entrepreneur, or new resident, this guide introduces you to the essentials that make Gulu City truly unique.
